Gaming

Here at BetterGaming we provide 1 on 1 training to people who want to improve at video games, whether thats to become an esports player or just improve overall understanding.

We have 3 different coaching levels specific to the gamers experience and needs. More information on the courses is provided on the info page.

PLANS

Beginner

This coaching is recommended for people who are new to video games, or have played casual single player games

Prices from: £10 an hour

Intermidiate

This coaching is recommened for people who have experience with video games, especially competetive games

Prices from: £15 an hour

Advanced

This coaching is recommended for skilled players who want to push their limits and be the best player they can

Prices from: £20 an hour
