General course information

Below is a general overview of the 3 seperate courses we teach. Make sure to get in contact if you are interested and we can discuss pricing and make sure to pick the best course for you.


Beginner course

By the end of the course, participants will have a solid understanding of how to start gaming, choose the right games for their interests, and engage with the gaming community.

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Format: Online
  • Prerequisites: None
  • Instructor: Experienced gamers and industry professionals

Intermediate course

The Intermediate Course to Gaming is designed for individuals who have a basic understanding of gaming principles and wish to deepen their knowledge and skills. This course covers a range of topics, including advanced gameplay strategies, game mechanics, the psychology of gaming, and an introduction to game development. Whether you aim to become a better player, understand the industry, or start creating your own games, this course provides the tools and insights you need.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Weekly Commitment: 4-6 hours
  • Format: Online or in-person (depending on provider)
  • Start Dates: Multiple start dates throughout the year

Advanced course

An advanced video game training course is designed for experienced gamers who want to refine their skills, learn advanced techniques, and possibly pursue a professional career in gaming or esports. These courses typically cover a range of topics, from in-depth gameplay strategies and mechanics to mental and physical conditioning.

  • Duration: 6 months (part-time), 3 months (full-time)
  • Format: Online and in-person options available
  • Start Dates: Quarterly intakes
